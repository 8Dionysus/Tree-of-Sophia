# AGENTS.md

This file applies to review/archive material under `ToS/public-compatibility/review/`.

## What lives here

`ToS/public-compatibility/review/` keeps superseded scaffold and pilot examples that are no
longer part of the active compatibility surface.

It holds:

- review/archive material for earlier bounded pilots
- superseded example clusters kept for lineage of review rather than live canon
- archived example packs such as `ToS/public-compatibility/review/calibration-family-pilot/`

## Editing posture

Treat these files as review/archive material. Active canon stays under
`ToS/canon/`.
Keep superseded surfaces visibly marked rather than silently deleted when they
still matter for review history.
Current validators and tiny-entry routes point at active compatibility surfaces.
A later pass that reactivates archived material should move or copy it into the
active owner route and validate that route.
