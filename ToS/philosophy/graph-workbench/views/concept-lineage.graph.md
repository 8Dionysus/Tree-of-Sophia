# Concept Lineage Graph View

## Lens

Shows concepts, semantic fields, problems, principles, contrasts, analogies,
and lineages across branches.

## Future Inputs

- concept nodes
- practice nodes
- corpus and work nodes
- `develops-from`, `parallel-to`, `contrasts-with`, and `commentary-on`
  relations

## Boundary

This view can compare branches. It should not flatten languages, genres,
institutions, or source posture into one universal term map.
