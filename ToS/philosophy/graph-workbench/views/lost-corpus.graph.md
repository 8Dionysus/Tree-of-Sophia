# Lost Corpus Graph View

## Lens

Shows lost, fragmentary, indirectly attested, or heavily mediated corpora and
the evidence routes that make them visible.

## Future Inputs

- corpus, preservation, edition, and controversy nodes
- source witnesses, quotations, catalogues, inscriptions, and later testimony
- `lost-but-attested-by`, `mediated-through`, `preserved-by`, and
  `evidence-for` relations

## Boundary

This view keeps absence visible. It should not fill lost corpora with invented
content just because later transmission proves that something once existed.
