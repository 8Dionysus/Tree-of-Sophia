# Ritual Law Graph View

## Lens

Shows treaty, oath, law, decree, ritual, legitimation, royal apology, and
state action as connected philosophical infrastructure.

## Future Inputs

- treaty, decree, ritual, institution, and concept nodes
- source witness and inscription nodes
- `legitimates`, `ritualizes`, `decreed-by`, `belongs-to`,
  `institutionalized-in`, and `contested-by` relations

## Boundary

This view can show law and ritual as thinking-in-action without collapsing the
branch into either modern legal history or generic religion.
