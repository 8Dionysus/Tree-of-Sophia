# Recurrence Roadmap

Recurrence is planted as calibration return. It helps ToS re-enter a route
after drift without inventing hidden continuity.

## Current Contour

- Keep calibration-return as the current part.
- Keep re-entry anchored to a source, branch, canon, review, or decision
  surface.
- Keep recurrence weaker than memory, runtime, and canon authority.

## Next Work

- Clarify re-entry when agents repeatedly lose the same ToS axis, branch route,
  or source-authority boundary.
- Return drift to the smallest owner surface that can repair it.

## When Time Comes

- Add payload when recurrence records become repeated reviewed objects.
- Split recurrence only if calibration return, branch re-entry, and source
  re-anchoring become distinct operations.

## Out Of Scope

Hidden scheduler, automatic recursor, canon rewrite, and source-core relocation.
