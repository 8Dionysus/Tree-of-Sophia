# Antifragility Roadmap

Antifragility is planted as ToS source-resilience posture. It helps the tree
turn stress into cleaner source, branch, and review routes instead of growing
by accumulation.

## Current Contour

- Keep source resilience as a review posture around weak source routes,
  flattening pressure, branch sprawl, and premature synthesis.
- Keep refusal and pruning language tied to source, branch, candidate, or canon
  owner surfaces.
- Keep this mechanic planted until repeated ToS cases need active
  source-resilience artifacts.
- Keep graph and atlas expansion honest when volume, novelty, or weak source
  posture pressures the tree toward premature synthesis.

## Next Work

- Name recurring bad-growth patterns only after they appear in real ToS review
  work.
- Return suspicious growth to source witness, branch review, candidate review,
  or canon restraint instead of opening a broad antifragility lane.

## When Time Comes

pruning, or re-entry shape.
