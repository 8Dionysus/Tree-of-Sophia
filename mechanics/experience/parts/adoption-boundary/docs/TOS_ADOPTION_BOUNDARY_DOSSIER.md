# TOS ADOPTION BOUNDARY DOSSIER

Status: candidate
Owner surface: `Tree-of-Sophia`
Part: `adoption-boundary`

## Purpose

How ToS material can inform dossiers without direct adoption.

## Core law

- Adoption must be explicit.
- Local owner consent is required.
- Durable behavior change needs evidence, rollback and retention.
- Tree-of-Sophia does not serve as a direct runtime adoption source.

## Lifecycle hooks

- request
- readiness
- shadow
- decision
- activation
- retention

## Outputs

- tos_adoption_boundary_dossier

## Stop lines

- No hidden assistant self-adoption.
- No adoption without local owner consent.
- No direct Tree-of-Sophia runtime write or runtime adoption.
- No KAG forced adoption into source repos.
- No routing layer authorship of meaning.
- No persistent change without rollback or explicit quarantine fallback.

## Notes

This document belongs to the adoption-boundary part. It assumes
candidate-review has accepted a shared pattern for review, but it refuses to
treat review acceptance as automatic adoption. Adoption is a second sovereign
act: local owner consent, compatibility, shadow proof, rollback path, retention
watch, and kind-safe projection are required.
