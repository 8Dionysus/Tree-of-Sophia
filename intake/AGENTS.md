# AGENTS.md

This file applies to candidate intake material under `intake/`.

## What lives here

`intake/` holds raw extracted candidate material that may later inform authored
ToS tree work.

Typical contents include:

- raw tables of candidate nodes
- raw tables of candidate relations
- pass-specific notes about bounded extraction work

## Editing posture

Keep `intake/` visibly provisional.
Material here is candidate material, not source authority and not canonical tree
law.
Do not treat `intake/` as a substitute for primary witness files in `sources/`
or authored node surfaces in `tree/`.
Keep the first wave light and path-oriented rather than turning `intake/` into a
full pipeline before real raw-table usage begins.
