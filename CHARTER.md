# ToS Charter

## Purpose

Tree of Sophia (ToS) is a source-first living knowledge architecture for philosophy and world thought.

This repository is the canonical public statement of that architecture. It exists to keep ToS rooted in explicit sources, lineage-aware structure, and human-reviewable synthesis even as adjacent operational and derived systems grow around it.

## Mission

ToS exists to support long-horizon cultivation of thought through:

- source-linked textual grounding
- concept and lineage tracing
- context-rich interpretation
- human-reviewed synthesis
- provenance-aware growth
- durable architectural clarity across time and traditions

## What this repository owns

This repository owns ToS-level truth about:

- what ToS is
- what kinds of nodes and relations belong in ToS
- what source-first discipline should remain stable
- how raw material, derived extraction, and human-reviewed synthesis stay distinct
- how ToS relates to AoA, `aoa-kag`, and `abyss-stack`

## What this repository does not own

This repository does not own:

- the AoA ecosystem constitution
- infrastructure deployment details
- KAG-derived projections as primary truth
- routing surfaces as such
- generic summaries detached from source traceability

## Core principles

- sources before abstraction
- lineages before isolated fragments
- human review before authority claims
- explicit provenance over floating interpretation
- plural scope without silent flattening
- growth through explicit structure

## Role in the broader ecosystem

Treat `Tree-of-Sophia` as:

- the source-authored knowledge architecture
- the public conceptual root of ToS
- the place where source-first meaning and structural discipline are named

Treat related repositories as follows:

- `Agents-of-Abyss` is the operational federation around ToS
- `aoa-kag` is the derived knowledge substrate built from authoritative sources
- `abyss-stack` is the runtime body beneath both

## Long-term direction

If ToS matures successfully, it should remain source-first even as derivative and operational systems become more capable.

The goal is not to dissolve authored meaning into infrastructure or retrieval machinery.

The goal is to cultivate a living, traceable architecture of thought.
